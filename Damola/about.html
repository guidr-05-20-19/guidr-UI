About Guidr

Guidr is an app that helps individuals log their trips and present them to potential employers.Users can show specific customized trips that they've led or taken part of.

Features

  • Users can login to their account and sign up using a username and password.
  • Users can add a tagline,title, age and length of time spent as a guide.
  • Users can view trips that they have on their portfolio.
  • Users can create, edit and delete past trips.
